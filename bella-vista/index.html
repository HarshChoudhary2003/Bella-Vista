Our Heritage

The Bella Vista Story

Founded in 1987 by the Rossi family, Bella Vista has been a beacon of authentic Italian dining. Our story began in a small kitchen in Florence where Nonna Giulia's handwritten recipes were first put to the test.

Today, under Chef Marco Rossi, we honour that same passion — celebrating tradition while embracing the finest modern techniques.

  • ✓ Ingredients imported weekly from Italy
  • ✓ All pasta and bread made fresh daily
  • ✓ Michelin-recognised culinary team
  • ✓ 500+ label Italian wine cellar
