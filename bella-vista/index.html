Our Heritage

The Bella Vista Story

Founded in 1987 by the Rossi family, Bella Vista has been a beacon of authentic Italian dining for over three decades. Our story began in a small kitchen in Florence, where Nonna Giulia's handwritten recipes were first put to the test.

Today, under the guidance of Executive Chef Marco Rossi, we carry that same passion into every dish — honouring tradition while embracing the finest modern techniques.

  • ✓ Ingredients imported weekly from Italy
  • ✓ All pasta and bread made fresh daily
  • ✓ Michelin-recognised culinary team
  • ✓ 500+ label Italian wine cellar
